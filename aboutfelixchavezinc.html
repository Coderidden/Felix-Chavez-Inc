Felix Chavez, Inc. was founded in 1982 by Felix O. Chavez, Chief Designer and Artist. Originally named Fine Art Decorating Company and in 1993 was replaced with our actual name.

Now we have the great honor to inform and state a brief history about one of the oldest established companies in the United States within the industry of restoration, conservation, and creation of new decorative design in old classical manner skills and all kinds of techniques in interior architectural features around New York State and across the country.

Our nearly 40 years as a company has allowed us to become the single most artful entity, unequaled in quality.

Our staff of artists and artisans have the highest level of study and experience.

This is the only company to provide an unparalled guarantee of 50 years minimum on the stability of our skills especially in new decorative finishes. No one else even mentions durability in addition to beauty.

This organization is unique and original because it is the creator of the opalescent technique which consist of transforming any modest material such as plaster elements, stone, metals, wood, poultry, etc. into a beautiful noble material; appropriate for modern and classical decoration.
